EVER THOUGHT OF HOW YOU CAN START A BUSINESS?

Heres How You Can Start A Business:

Starting a small business can feel big at first, but if you break it down into steps, it becomes much easier.
Here’s a simple roadmap you can follow:
1. Come Up With an Idea
Think about what you’re good at or what people around you need.
Examples: baking snacks, tutoring, selling crafts, offering computer help, or reselling clothes.
2. Do Some Research
Who will buy your product/service? (friends, neighbors, students, etc.)
Who else is selling it? Can you do it better, cheaper, or more uniquely?
3. Make a Small Plan
Decide what you’ll sell, how much you’ll charge, and where you’ll sell (online, at school, local market, etc.).
Write down your costs (ingredients, materials, tools) and how much profit you’ll make.
4. Start Small
You don’t need lots of money to begin—start with what you have.
For example, if you want to sell cookies, bake a small batch and test them with friends.
5. Spread the Word
Tell friends and family.
Use free social media like WhatsApp, Instagram, or TikTok to show what you’re selling.
Word of mouth is powerful—treat your first customers well!
6. Manage Your Money
Keep business money separate from personal money.
Always write down sales, costs, and profits.
7. Grow Slowly
Once you see demand, reinvest some of your profit to make more products or improve your service.
Listen to customer feedback and adjust.
